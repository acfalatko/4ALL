Problem Statement


Institutions, products and websites do not always meet accessibility requirements which makes it difficult for disabled communities to participate; of those institutions, products, and websites that do satisfy a minimum criteria of accessibility standards, only few get in touch directly towards disabled communities.

We provide access to accessible avenues.


The purpose of our organization is to test and verify websites, institutions, and products for their levels of accessibility; more importantly, our goal is to aggregate those that satisfy certain accessibility standards onto a platform specifically for users with disabilities. This allows for these communities to locate information and activities that are designed with them in mind. We also provide access to a catalogue of helpful resources for disabled communities.
